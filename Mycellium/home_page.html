    abandoned land in San Francisco!

    We want to rehabilitate an abandoned parking lot into a garden so that not only is helping the community but helping the environment. Creating a garden will help with flooding* when it rains and heat inequity caused by the concrete*. We also want to create a space that is for the people in the community. Growing native plants and our own produce can help people in the community who are food insecure. Not only will growing native plants and produce help distribute food to the community but it will also brighten up the street.
