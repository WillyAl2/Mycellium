    What is the project?

    Vacant Space Garden

    Using garden beds and short rooted plants, rehabilitating an abandoned space with native plants and produce. Creation of a communal space for community members. Success would be measured by the amount of greenery provided to a space that is otherwise contributing to heat inequity and food insecurity.


    Food cabinet & garden voluntaring

    food cabinet could be Part II of the vacant space rehab, produce from the garden would be made available for folks who are food insecure. Canned/processed foods will also be made available. Success would be measured by contributions and build out of a network of farmers who can contribute bruised or “ugly” produce to this initiative.


    Coastal clean up.

    Identity areas that require the most clean up/support with restoration (check in with coastal protection). Find alternative areas to support with replanting/restoration and clean up. Acquire whatever training is needed to support.
